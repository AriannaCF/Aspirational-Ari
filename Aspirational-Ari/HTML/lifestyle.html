Notes:

Last night I dreamt that I was on a beach vacation. My room was just feet away from the ocean, but three days into my week long vacation I still hadn't been to the beach. Late that night, I decided that it was time to step out into the sun and enjoy the rest of my vacation. While I was changing into my bathing suit, my mother came into my room to ask me what I was doing. I told her that I was going to the beach, and she started telling me not to go, saying that there were too many tourists, the lifeguards were telling people not to swim, and a number of other excuses. For a moment I reconsidered my choice, but then I looked out of my window at the bright sun and the crashing waves, and the call of the ocean once more overcame me. I told her that I didn't care about any of that, and that I wanted to do what I had come on vacation to do, which is to bathe in the warmth of the sun and swim in the ocean. When I awoke, I knew it was time to begin.
